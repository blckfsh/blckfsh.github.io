Exam Generator

When you post a job opening for your company, there will be interested individual that wants to apply for the role. In order for you to test if they are qualified, the employer will send an exam and usually via email. There are times, that the role is so in-demand, the employer will find himself/herself sending a lot of exams thru email. The goal of this app is to make the sending of exams thru email automated. The employer needs only to input the applicant details, and the app will take care of the rest as it is programmed to send an email with the exam attach once the employer input the details.

  • Created - Oct 2021
  • technologies used - React, Nodejs, Mongodb, Nodemailer
  • role - Frontend, Backend
